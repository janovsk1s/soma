The bundled model is `ggml-tiny-q5_1.bin`, 32,152,673 bytes. It was obtained
from the [whisper.cpp model repository](https://huggingface.co/ggerganov/whisper.cpp/blob/c521a4b02f422512d734391fdf08bb08c0862f68/ggml-tiny-q5_1.bin).
Its recorded source and digest also live beside it in
`whisper/src/main/assets/MODEL_INFO.txt`. A release must not replace the model
unless that record, this notice, and the reviewed digest are updated together.

The compact gzip-encoded `eu_food_catalog.bin` asset contains food names, energy,
protein, carbohydrate and fat values per 100 g, plus selected Fineli portion
masses. It is generated by `tools/generate_eu_food_catalog.py` and has SHA-256
`bdc5ea377cb2155e934501ebbaad9b1ab9303310a585ba117946ba43a4198d78`. Fineli requires
attribution to “Finnish Institute for Health and Welfare, Fineli”. Ciqual
requires the explicit source “Anses. 2025. Table de composition nutritionnelle
des aliments Ciqual 2025. https://doi.org/10.57745/RDMHWY”. Values remain
subject to the source datasets’ limitations and are presented by Soma as
official averages, never as package-label measurements.
